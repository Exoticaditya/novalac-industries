FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What makes Novalac paints different from other brands?

Novalac Industries offers premium quality paints with superior coverage, durability, and eco-friendly formulations. Our products are ISO certified and backed by 25+ years of industry expertise. We use advanced technology and high-grade raw materials to ensure long-lasting, vibrant finishes.

How much area does one liter of paint cover?

Coverage varies by product type. Generally, our emulsion paints cover 120-140 sq.ft per liter per coat on properly prepared surfaces. Wall putty covers approximately 15-20 sq.ft per kg. For accurate estimates, please consult our technical data sheets or contact our support team.

Are Novalac paints eco-friendly and safe?

Yes! Our paints are formulated with low VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) content, making them safe for indoor use. They are lead-free, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly. We comply with all national and international safety standards.

What is the warranty period for Novalac products?

We offer a 5-7 year warranty on our premium emulsion paints and 3-5 years on specialty products, subject to proper surface preparation and application as per our guidelines. Warranty terms vary by product category.

How do I choose the right paint for my project?

Consider factors like surface type (interior/exterior), climate conditions, desired finish (matte/glossy), and budget. Our product categories are designed for specific applications. Contact our technical team for personalized recommendations based on your requirements.

Do you provide color matching services?

Yes! We offer custom color matching services. You can bring a sample or choose from our extensive shade card with 500+ colors. Our universal stainer allows precise color customization for any project.

Where can I buy Novalac products?

Our products are available through our network of authorized dealers across India. Use our Dealer Locator above to find the nearest dealer in your area. For bulk orders, contact us directly.

Do you offer technical support for application?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive technical support including application guidelines, surface preparation advice, and troubleshooting assistance. Our team is available 24/7 to help ensure perfect results for your project.
